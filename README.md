# ✈️ British Airways Reviews Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **British Airways Reviews Dashboard** is an interactive Tableau visualization designed to analyze customer feedback and measure passenger satisfaction across multiple service dimensions. The dashboard transforms raw review data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, identify improvement opportunities, and enhance the overall customer experience.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* 📊 Monitor overall customer satisfaction levels.
* ✈️ Evaluate performance across various airline services.
* 📈 Analyze rating trends over time.
* 🌍 Compare customer experiences across countries and regions.
* 🛫 Assess aircraft-specific performance.
* 💡 Support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🔑 Dashboard Features

### ⭐ KPI Summary Cards

Provides a quick overview of key performance metrics:

* ⭐ **Average Rating:** 4.2
* 💺 **Seat Comfort:** 2.9
* 👨‍✈️ **Cabin Staff Service:** 3.3
* 🍽️ **Food & Beverages:** 2.4
* 🧳 **Ground Service:** 3.0
* 💷 **Value for Money:** 2.8
* 🎬 **Entertainment:** 1.4

---

### 📈 Average Rating by Month

A time-series visualization showing customer satisfaction trends over time.

**Benefits:**

* Identify seasonal trends.
* Track service performance changes.
* Detect periods of customer dissatisfaction.

---

### 🌍 Average Rating by Country

Interactive geographic map displaying customer ratings across countries.

**Benefits:**

* Compare regional performance.
* Identify high- and low-performing markets.
* Understand global customer sentiment.

---

### ✈️ Average Rating by Aircraft

Aircraft-level performance analysis comparing ratings across different aircraft models.

**Benefits:**

* Evaluate passenger satisfaction by aircraft type.
* Identify best-performing aircraft.
* Support fleet and service improvement initiatives.

---

### 📊 Number of Reviews by Aircraft

Displays review volume for each aircraft model.

**Benefits:**

* Validate rating reliability.
* Highlight aircraft with the most passenger feedback.
* Provide context for performance comparisons.

---
